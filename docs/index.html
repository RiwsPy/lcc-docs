Shar-Teel BG2EE
🟢✅😀

BG2EE

EET

Lava Del'Vortel

Shar-Teel for BG2EE does exactly what the mod's title suggests: it allows players to meet and recruit Shar-Teel during Baldur's Gate II: Enhanced Edition's campaign. The character comes with all dialogues (with the PC, other companions, and interjections).
The mod also comes with a 'companions with benefits' relationship with the main character, provided the PC is a male of any non-good alignment (evil or lawful/chaotic/true neutral), with Strength and Charisma 10+.
You may meet her in Bodhi's Lair, no matter who you aligned with: Bodhi or the Shadow Thieves. Know, however, that the path you took affects her dialogue and her kit, as she may start either as a Deathly Reaver (gains a bonus vs. humans, level drain immunity, and may cast Vampiric Touch) or as a Hot-Blooded Reaver (gains a bonus vs. undead, a great bonus to saving throws vs. death, and may cast Haste on herself). Regardless of your choice, she also gains another minor ability that matches her dislike of men.

Incompatible with: Angelo NPC for BGII, Sharteel NPC for BG2

Lichdom
🟢✅😀

SoD

BG2EE

EET

Daniel Valle

Across realms and planes, many have already become Liches or Half-Liches in one way or another. Perhaps they sought immortality. Perhaps they were tired of having to eat and breathe. Maybe they were tired of being frozen, stunned, sick, entangled, drained, paralyzed, petrified, poisoned, polymorphing into a squirrel, stunned... Whatever their reasoning, with this mod, you too can become a Lich with the help of Gravetender Morris in the Athkatla Graveyard District (SoA) or in Saradush (ToB).
But wanting it isn't enough. You have to pay the price (120,000 PO), be at least level 12, have the right alignment, pass a series of quests and so on. To become a lich for good, you need to be physically immune to non-magical weapons, poison, cold and electricity... See the readme for more details.

The mod features a whole host of components for configuring the lich you want to be.

Jazon the revenant
🟢✅😀

BG2EE

EET

IWDEE

Deratiseur

Jazon the revenant is a mute undead warrior who can follow you as a 7th member of the party. He is level 8 and will never progress, but he types hard and resists everything (strength and constitution 19, undead immunities, 75% resistance to everything and fast regeneration).

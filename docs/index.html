Elai NPC
🟢✅😀

BG2

Andyr

This mod adds Elai, a Lawful Evil male Half-elf Ranger to the game as a joinable NPC. He can be found in the Temple Ruins in the Umar Hills. I would recommend going there before departing for Spellhold, though it is not essential you will miss out on some stuff if you don't... If starting from ToB you can summon him from the Fate Spirit as with other NPCs. He is not a romance character, though does interact with the PC and other party members and you can force-talk him in the style of the FWP Flirt Packs to ask him questions. He has his own unique kit: Shadovar Scout, which allows him to perform backstabs, and he has access to mage spells instead of ranger spells.
STR 15, DEX 16, CON 14, INT 17, WIS 10, CHA 13.

This is a mod that allows casting level bonuses from various sources to accumulate. By default, Casting Level bonuses in the IE games do not stack -- the last-applied effect always wins. ⚠️❌😀

BGT

Starn

This mod increases hostel prices by taking the following factors into account: location, size, competition, standing, security. As a result, it becomes much more difficult to spend the night in the cities, unless you want to sleep in the sewers. Room categories have also been redefined. An inn that looks like a cheap bar no longer offers the Noble or Royal room category. Other inns, which visually have no rooms at all, can no longer accommodate overnight stays and have been transformed into taverns.

EET

IWDEE

Deratiseur

Jazon the revenant is a mute undead warrior who can follow you as a 7th member of the party. He is level 8 and will never progress, but he types hard and resists everything (strength and constitution 19, undead immunities, 75% resistance to everything and fast regeneration). Aura NPC for Baldur's Gate: Enhanced Edition
🟢✅😀

BGEE

SoD

BG2EE

EET

AionZ

This mod allows you to recruit the gnome Aurelia Minerva Eveline Glimmershine. In BG1, you'll find her in the center of Beregost, in BG2, you'll find her in Waukeen's Promenade right after attending an event at Jan's house. As an artificer (thief profile), Aura can modify certain magical items that the party acquires on their journey.
Gentle and idealistic at heart, she can be rather shy and socially inept when interacting with strangers, but easily becomes enthusiastic and excitable when the subject strays from her areas of expertise, being able to talk or (more likely) lecture others for hours on end. Aura is generally friendly, making at least an effort to bond with all her fellow travelers, with varying degrees of success. She approves of innovation, seeking knowledge and using her talents to help others. Consequently, she is best suited to parties of good alignment, although she can follow any PC of at least average level.
A female PC of any race can flirt with Aura. A compatible PC gains romantic dialog options during her friendship chats, but there is no romance.
D In SoD, Aura can start a romance with a non-evil female of any race with at least 12 Intelligence and 10 Charisma. She is generally affectionate and will respond amicably to a partner who is friendly, intelligent and willing to listen. On the other hand, she's quick to walk away if the PC shows no sign of interest, and blunt responses are quick to drive her away.
STR 8, DEX 16, CON 13, INT 19, WIS 14, CHA 15.
